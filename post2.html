Accelerated use of Microsoft Dynamics NAV within the Antenna Company B.V.

Situation

  • I was working as an intern in a small tech-company in the reputed high-tech campus of Eindhoven, in close vicinity of Philips Research Eindhoven (Headquarters). I went in with a hands-on problem-solving mentality to find the best possible solution to problems they threw at me. I truly wanted to deliver a working-solution and not just a concept because I did that in my previous internship where I delivered a concept of a custom CRM with mock-ups. Besides my assignment was quite clear to me. I had an implementation project for five stakeholders at hand and I had to satisfy the requirements of each, including CEO and COO.
  • My skills at that time were mostly based on engineering and business processes. Facilitation skills like holding workshops, stakeholder-management, and working with very senior individuals in rank were required. Further analysis of the initial requirements brought up the technical feasibility of the task, not to mention the associated costs. I also had to work within deadlines. I had 90 working days to deliver.
  • It was one the most intense learning opportunities for me as I was about to pivot quite heavily towards the path I am on right now. It created a looming feeling of having much more to do than just solving the problems. This feeling lasted until the very end of my MSc. program in the UE and it have been the driving force of my formal education ever-since.
